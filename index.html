Top 3 shoe companies in the world

Nike

  • Country: US
  • Market value: $117.38 billion
  • According to the latest data, Nike is currently the biggest shoe company. As of December 2024, the popular sportswear brand is valued at $117.38 billion—nearly 1.5 times its valuation a decade ago in 2014, which was recorded at $83.07 billion. In 2023, Nike’s global revenue totaled $47.78 billion, more than double the $21.43 billion generated by second-placed Adidas.

Adidas

  • Country: Germany
  • Market value:$43.83 billion
  • Nike’s biggest rival, Adidas, is in second place with a market cap of $43.83 billion, which is around one-third of Nike’s. A closer look at these top two shoe companies shows that while Nike’s market cap has fallen by 28.96% since the end of 2023, Adidas has increased its company value by 20.68%. "

Deckers Brands

  • Country: US
  • Market value: $30.82 billion
  • The third-biggest shoe company is California-based Deckers Brands, which owns popular footwear brands like UGG and Teva. It is currently valued at $30.82 billion, marking a 79.2% increase from the end of 2023."
